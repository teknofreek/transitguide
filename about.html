About Us

Doha/Qatar's first integrated public transit App. Find routes, stops and timetables for bus & metro rail (upcoming) and arrivals / departures near you; Explore bus routes and metro lines (upcoming), and plan your navigation across Qatar using our Journey planner. Contains comprehensive information about Doha/Qatar's public bus and metro services (upcoming) including routes, stops, maps and timetables/schedules. Also has an integrated trip/journey planner with location search functionality which combines all transport options to help you figure out the most efficient routes & travel mode to reach your destination with the expected duration of travel. Our endeavor is to promote Sustainable Urban Mobility & Accessibility through the use of Public Transport. Feel free to share your feedback/comments/suggestions so that we can enhance the App further.
